On August 18th, 2023,
I moved from here to here for college. I'm from a small suburb near Seattle, Washington, and the transition was more drastic than I was prepared for. It was a big move, and I didn't know anyone on the East Coast. I didn't even know anything ABOUT the East Coast. During this time I experienced several instances of culture shock, cuz it's just so different from where I came from. I wasn't expecting it at all, but there it was.
I guess you could call this my East Coast Culture Shock.
