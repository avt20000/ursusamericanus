One Page Pitch
WHAT??
I’m from Washington State. This is on the complete other side of the United States. I think something that people tend to forget is that America is enormous. It’s faster and cheaper for me to fly to Europe from NYC than it is for me to fly home. The vastness of America, I think, has led to each state and major city to develop its own culture, identity, and social fabric. The point I’m trying to make with this is that I experienced a very unique culture shock when moving from coast to coast, which I didn’t expect. Even though I’m still in the same country, everything is completely different. My entire way of life went through a transition over the past year.
WHY??
I want to document everything that I found strange, new, or had to adjust to since moving here. I think it’s important to demonstrate the culture of where I come from and compare it with the culture I’m trying to integrate into.
WHO??
My target audience would be other people around my age range, or honestly anyone making a big change in their life. I want these new things to feel more benign, and open them up to discussion so that people don’t feel so alone about it..
Guiding Principles
Handmade Aesthetic
This is my experience. I want the aesthetic of the website to be completely mine. I want to achieve this by layering a lot of hand drawn elements such as images and typefaces. This is important to me because I need the website to be reflective of my personal experience, and effectively tell the story I want to communicate.
Accessibility
The website needs to document several stories that come together to form a bigger picture. The flow, readability, and navigability of the website all need to be extremely accessible and simple to understand. This is important because it will prevent the hindrance bad accessibility can have on effective storytelling.
Engagement with Story
This should be a fun website. It needs to be interactive, engaging, and keep users wanting to see the next installment. This is important because a good story needs to keep users engaged, so the design of the website should be suited towards interactivity and interest.
